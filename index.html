What Is the Nigeria Bar Association Lagos State ?

The Nigerian Bar Association (NBA) Lagos Branch is a professional, non-profit association which is comprised of all lawyers called to the Nigerian Bar and practicing in the Lagos Division of the Lagos High Court of Justice. The Lagos Divisionencompasses Lagos Island, Lekki, Ikoyi, Victoria Island, Apapa, and Surulere. The Nigerian Bar Association (NBA) Lagos Branch is under the umbrella Nigerian Bar Association which is made up of the different branches of NBA across the statesin Nigeria.
